Refund/Return/Cancellations Policy

Last updated Oct 08, 2022



The following condition applies to the refund of the amount you have paid

1.Once your shopping order has been cancelled with any reason, the refund amount will be credited back to the source account within 6-10 working days. In the case of certain public sector banks, it can take up to 10-15 working days.

What should I do if I don’t get my refund in the promised time?
2. We work quickly to make sure your refund gets to you on time. On the off-chance that it's been delayed, please get in touch with us here. Don't forget to keep your order number and email address handy.
Why do I see a disabled cancel button ?
3.That means the product(s) from your order have already been delivered and you can't make any cancellations now.

Can i return the product after delivered ?
4.Yes! Returns are only possible on all items except undergarments.we cannot accept returns after 10 days from the date of delivery for non-electronic items and 7 days for electronic products.

Can I return a part of my order ?
5.Yes you can. Just select the product(s) you want to return and initiate the return right away. You should know that we can’t accept returns of incomplete product combos (for example: Buy one get one free combos, free gifts, etc.).

6.Can I cancel my return request and choose to keep the product ?
Sorry, but you can’t ‘cancel’ a return request. Here’s what you can do – at the time of pick-up, make it clear that you’d like to keep the product.

This is not what I ordered. How do I replace it ?
7.If your order or a part of it does not match the product description, we'll look into it right away. You can initiate a return request though My Orders in the My Account section.

8.Refunds are only available after the product has been returned to us (return address will be printed on each product, or contact us)

9.For a refund or product return you can use rturn and cancell option in app or contact with us contact.selloutday@gmail.com or +918592025026 at whatsapp.
